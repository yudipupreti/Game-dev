Yudip Upreti is the founder and CEO of Yuwadevs, a mobile game development company based in Nepal. With a passion for gaming and a deep knowledge of programming, Yudip Upreti founded Yuwadevs in February 2022 and has since led the company in the development of several popular mobile games. Prior to founding Yuwadevs, Yudip Upreti worked as a software engineer and web developer, gaining valuable experience in the technology industry. He is committed to driving innovation in the mobile gaming industry and is dedicated to making Yuwadevs a leading name in the field.
