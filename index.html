Yuwadevs


Yuwadevs - A rapidly growing game development company based in Nepal. Founded by Yudip Upreti in February 2022, Yuwadevs has quickly established itself as a major player in the mobile gaming industry. The company has developed several popular mobile games that are available on the Google Play Store, including 2D Cops Chase, Pyxel Gurkhas, Infinite Space Racer, Cube Box, and Zulus Adventure. With a focus on creating innovative and engaging games, Yuwadevs is poised to become a leading name in the world of mobile gaming.
